LEGEND KRAPFEN - THE PUNK
============================================

INTERNAL ID: krapfen (Tag: legend_krapfen)
DISPLAY NAME: Krapfen, The Punk

--- COMMANDS ---

1. GET SPAWN TOME (Admin):
   /function minilegends:bosses/give_krapfen_tome

2. SUMMON DIRECTLY (Admin):
   /function minilegends:bosses/krapfen

--- RECIPE ---
Crafting Table (Shaped):
G = Gold Block
P = Porkchop
E = Emerald Block
H = Piglin Head

[ G ][ P ][ G ]
[ P ][ E ][ P ]
[ G ][ H ][ G ]

Result: 1x Tome of Krapfen

--- BOSS STATS ---
Type: Piglin
HP: 300
Armor: Full Golden Armor (Protection 10)
Weapon: "Kingslayer" (Sharpness X, Fire Aspect III, Reach 11)

--- ABILITIES ---

1. KING STEALER (Passive)
   - 40% chance on hit to steal one piece of the player's armor.
   - The armor is removed from the player and dropped on the ground.

2. DECAYING (Passive)
   - Inflicts Wither VIII for 2 seconds on every hit.

3. PIGLIN SWARM (Phase)
   - Summons 10 helper Piglins every 20% HP loss (at 240, 180, 120, 60 HP).
   - Helper Stats:
     - Weapon: Golden Sword (Sharpness 8, Knockback 7, Fire Aspect 2, Reach 15)
     - Armor: Golden Helmet (Projectile Protection 10)
